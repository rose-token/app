How It Works

1

Deposit

Send USDC to the vault and receive ROSE tokens at the current NAV. The vault automatically diversifies into BTC, Gold, and stablecoins.

2

Redeem

Burn ROSE tokens to withdraw USDC at the current exchange rate. The ROSE price reflects total treasury value.

3

Asset Backing

Each ROSE token is backed by real-world assets: 40% Bitcoin, 40% Gold (PAXG), 20% USDC. As asset prices change, so does your position.
